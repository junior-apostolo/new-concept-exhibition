About Us

Founded in 2020, New Concept Exhibition is focused on producing new and modern designs that elevate and influence the way your company is seen by the public. We are committed to bring our clients ideas to light by utilizing high quality materials delivering better results to the final product.

With connections from the entire US territory, our personnel are constantly searching for new methods of innovating the market and are ready for the next challenge.

Services

We provide all your needs to make your booth look perfect and a successful event.

Design

We can bring your ideas to life with our experienced and creative designers.

Construction

Once the design is approved, we will start producing your booth and in just a few days you will be able to see your project ready, being able to make any adjustments if necessary.

Installation/Dismantling

We will transport and install your booth in the venue and once the event is over, we will dismantle everything

Storage

After the event is over, we will bring the materials back to our warehouse and if you wish to use the same booth in any future event, we can store it for you and we will make sure it will be ready for the next show (fees may apply).

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

These are our most frequently asked questions:

  • What is your typical lead time?

    We ask for a contractual confirmation 45-30 days before the event, after that withing 2-3 weeks we will have your booth built and ready to go. We do accept projects within less than 30 days before the event, but prices may vary

  • Which other states/countries do you also operate?

    We can operate in all the states in the US, except Hawaii and Alaska. We also operate in Canada, Mexico, Brazil, UK and Spain (if your country is not listed, please contact us for further information).

  • What types of booths do you work with?

    We make custom built booths utilizing plywood and we also offer the Octanorm line for a lower budget.

  • What does your price include?

    Depending on your needs we offer a design and everything that goes in the booth, from the structure itself to the flooring, electrical and furniture.
